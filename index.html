Emergency Info

Signs of overdose: unresponsive, shallow breathing, blue lips/fingertips.

Steps:

  1. Call 911 immediately (Tap to Call)
  2. Administer Narcan if available
  3. Perform rescue breathing if trained
  4. Stay with the person until help arrives
