About Us

PLURderapolis is the digital evolution of a harm reduction movement built from years of underground, grassroots work. Our roots go back as Project Peach who showed up with info tables, Narcan, and safety planning at local underground events.

We‚Äôve worked booths, kept people safe from overdoses, and heard the darkest stories people never wanted to tell. Back then, we kept track of known abusers in the scene. That shaped how we listen and act now‚Äîhelping people spot red flags and protect each other before it gets that far.

Now, we focus on the online spaces where the scene lives, so harm prevention and healing can reach more people faster.

Quick Resources

  • Need Narcan? Message us or check local orgs like Southside Harm Reduction or Steve Rummler HOPE Network.
  • Having a crisis? Reach out to 988 or use dontcallthepolice.com to find alternative support.
  • Worried your drugs are fake? Test everything. Carry strips. Ask around.
  • In an unsafe scene situation? DM us. We know how to move carefully and discreetly.
