Emergency Response

If Someone Is Unresponsive

1. Check for responsiveness - Tap shoulder and shout

2. Call 911 immediately - Minnesota's Good Samaritan Law provides protection

3. Check breathing - Look for chest movement

4. Administer Narcan - If available and trained

5. Perform rescue breathing - If not breathing

6. Place in recovery position - If breathing

About PLURderapolis

Our Mission

PLURderapolis is a grassroots harm reduction initiative serving Minnesota's electronic music community. Founded by and for ravers, we provide evidence-based resources, education, and support to promote safer nightlife experiences.

Our work is rooted in the PLUR ethos: Peace, Love, Unity, Respect. We believe in meeting people where they're at without judgment, reducing stigma, and empowering our community with knowledge and tools to make informed decisions about their health and safety.

We collaborate with local organizations, venues, and event promoters to distribute Narcan, test strips, and harm reduction information at shows, festivals, and community events throughout the Twin Cities metro area.

Our Values

  • Compassion over judgment
  • Evidence-based education
  • Community empowerment
  • Racial and social equity
  • Confidentiality and trust

Our Services

  • Narcan distribution & training
  • Fentanyl/xylazine test strips
  • Harm reduction education
  • Peer support & resources
  • Community outreach
