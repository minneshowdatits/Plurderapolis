About PLURderapolis

PLURderapolis is a harm reduction initiative serving the Minnesota community. We provide education, resources, and support to promote safer substance use and mental health practices.

Our mission is to build a healthier, safer nightlife and festival community through non-judgmental education and practical harm reduction strategies.
