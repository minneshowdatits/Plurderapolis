üö® Emergency Overdose Response

If Someone is Unresponsive:

  1. Check responsiveness - Tap shoulder and shout
  2. Call 911 immediately - Say "person not breathing"
  3. Check breathing - Look for chest movement
  4. Administer Narcan - If available and trained
  5. Rescue breathing - If not breathing
  6. Recovery position - If breathing

Overdose Signs:

  • Unresponsive to stimuli
  • Slow or no breathing
  • Pale or clammy skin
  • Blue lips or fingertips
  • Gurgling sounds

Minnesota Good Samaritan Law:

You will not be prosecuted for drug possession if you call 911 for an overdose. Your call could save a life.
