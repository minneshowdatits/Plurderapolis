ü´Ä Suspected Overdose (Minnesota Protocol)

  1. Check responsiveness - Shake and shout
  2. Call 911 immediately - MN Good Samaritan Law protects you
  3. Check breathing - Look for chest movement
  4. Administer Narcan - Spray one dose in nostril
  5. Rescue breathing - If not breathing: 30 compressions, 2 breaths
  6. Repeat Narcan - Every 2-3 minutes if no response
  7. Stay with person - Until help arrives
