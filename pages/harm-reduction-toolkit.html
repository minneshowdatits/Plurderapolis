🌈 Harm Reduction Toolkit

This toolkit exists so you can party smart and get home safe.

Before the event:

  • Eat a full, balanced meal
  • Bring a reusable water bottle (with electrolytes if possible)
  • Set your intentions and your boundaries — write them down if needed
  • Designate a check-in buddy or use a group safety chat
  • Test your substances with a reagent or fent strip

During the event:

  • Hydrate — sip, don’t chug
  • Take breaks to cool down and rest your body
  • Use gum, chapstick, and sunglasses if needed
  • Carry naloxone, earplugs, condoms, lube, and wipes
  • Don't mix drugs unless you understand the combo (check our 🧬 tool)

After the event:

  • Eat real food, sleep, and take a shower (trust us)
  • Journal or voice memo your experience
  • Don’t isolate if you're feeling off — reach out
  • Test leftover substances if you didn’t before
