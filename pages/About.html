About Us

PLURderapolis is a Minnesota-based harm reduction collective dedicated to supporting safety, education, and care within rave, festival, and nightlife communities.

Our journey began as Project Peach MN, running grassroots harm reduction booths at local events—distributing naloxone, testing supplies, earplugs, and sharing peer support. Those early moments shaped our mission and fueled our passion.

Today, PLURderapolis is a year-round digital resource hub, offering tools like polydrug interaction guides, safe supply maps, overdose response instructions, and consent-focused education.

This work is peer-led and grounded in lived experience—users, survivors, activists, and community members committed to honest, nonjudgmental support.

PLURderapolis exists to meet people where they are, with compassion, autonomy, and real tools to reduce harm and save lives.

Our Mission

  • Provide accessible, peer-driven harm reduction education and resources
  • Normalize conversations about consent, personal agency, and drug safety
  • Equip individuals with practical tools for safer nightlife and festival experiences
  • Foster community built on trust, respect, and shared lived knowledge
