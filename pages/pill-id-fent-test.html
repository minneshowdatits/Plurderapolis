💊 Pill ID & Fentanyl Testing

If you’re gonna take something, make sure it’s what you *think* it is. This guide can help you test it — or throw it out.

🕵️‍♀️ Step 1: Pill Identification

  • Use Drugs.com Imprint Checker — enter shape, color, and numbers
  • Sketchy pressies or “designer” pills? There’s no reliable visual ID — TEST IT
  • If it’s chalky, crumbly, or bitter AF: proceed with extreme caution

🧪 Step 2: Fentanyl Test Strip Use

  1. Crush a very small amount of the pill into a clean container
  2. Add about 1mL of clean water (roughly ¼ teaspoon)
  3. Stir until dissolved
  4. Dip the fent strip in for 15 seconds (blue side down)
  5. Lay it flat and wait 2–5 minutes

❗ How to read the result:

  • 1 line = Positive (fentanyl is present — DANGER)
  • 2 lines = Negative (no fentanyl detected — not a guarantee of safety)
  • Even a faint second line means negative

✦ Don’t test in dirty water. Don’t test a full pill. Always use a clean surface. ✦
