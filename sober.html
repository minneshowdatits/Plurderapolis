🕊️ Sober Ravers & Community

Why go sober? Enjoy the music, dance, and vibes fully aware — plus, it’s safer and helps your body and mind recover.

🎉 Local Sober Raver Events & Venues (Minnesota-Focused)
  • Sober Rave Nights at The Exchange (Minneapolis): Monthly sober dance parties with DJ sets, chill zones, and mixers. IG page.
  • Sober Glow Meetups (Twin Cities): Hosted by MN Sober Ravers Collective. Casual meetups focused on connection, meditation, and sober fun. Facebook Group.
  • The Sanctuary (Minneapolis): Sober‑friendly space offering alcohol-free dance events and wellness workshops. sanctuarympls.org.
💬 Sober Raver Social Groups & Support
  • Sober Rave Discord MN – Peer support space for sober ravers to share events, tips, or just hang. Ask via MN Sober Ravers Facebook group.
  • Meetup: Minnesota Sober Dance & Wellness – Group organizing sober hikes, yoga, dance parties. Search on Meetup.com.
  • Minnesota Recovery Connection – Peer-led support org hosting sober events and recovery resources. mnrecovery.org.
  • SMART Recovery Minnesota – Evidence-based meetings across metro/suburbs. smartrecovery.org/minnesota.
  • Celebrate Recovery at Grace Church – Inclusive group offering social events & sponsor networks. gracechurchmpls.org.
  • Adult Children of Alcoholics (ACA) – MN Chapter – For healing from family-related trauma. adultchildren.org.
  • Alano Club of Minneapolis – Sober social club w/ daily AA/NA/Al‑Anon meetings. alanoclubmpls.org.
  • Minnesota Harm Reduction Action Center – Occasionally hosts sober space circles. mnharc.org.
🍹 Top-Rated Mocktail Recipes
  • Electric Lemonade: Lemonade, NA blue curaçao syrup, sparkling water, lemon slice. Glows under blacklight.
  • Ginger Citrus Fizz: Ginger beer, lime juice, orange, mint. Zesty + energizing.
  • Virgin Mojito: Lime, mint, sugar, soda water. Sparkling + hydrating.
  • Cucumber Cooler: Cucumber juice, lime, simple syrup, tonic water. Crisp AF.
