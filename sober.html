Top-Rated Mocktail Recipes

Electric Lemonade
  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Pour 6 oz lemonade
  3. Add 1 oz non-alcoholic blue curaçao syrup
  4. Top with 2 oz sparkling water
  5. Stir gently
  6. Garnish with a lemon slice

Bonus: glows under blacklight!

Ginger Citrus Fizz
  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Add 4 oz ginger beer
  3. Squeeze 1 oz fresh lime juice
  4. Add 1 oz fresh orange juice
  5. Drop in a few fresh mint leaves
  6. Stir lightly and garnish with an orange wedge
Virgin Mojito
  1. Muddle 8 fresh mint leaves with 1 tsp sugar in a glass
  2. Add juice of 1 lime
  3. Fill the glass with ice
  4. Top with soda water
  5. Stir well and garnish with a mint sprig and lime wedge
PLUR Punch
  1. Mix 4 oz cranberry juice with 2 oz pineapple juice
  2. Add 1 oz fresh lime juice
  3. Fill with ice and top with club soda
  4. Garnish with lime wheel and edible glitter

Perfect for rave-themed gatherings!
