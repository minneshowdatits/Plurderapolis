🕊️ Sober Ravers & Community

Why go sober? Enjoy the music, dance, and vibes fully aware — plus, it’s safer and helps your body and mind recover.

🎉 Local Sober Raver Events & Venues (Minnesota-Focused)

  • Sober Rave Nights at The Exchange (Minneapolis): Monthly sober dance parties with DJ sets, chill zones, and mixers. Check their IG page.
  • Sober Glow Meetups (Twin Cities): Hosted by MN Sober Ravers Collective. Casual meetups focused on connection, meditation, and sober fun. See their Facebook Group.
  • The Sanctuary (Minneapolis): Sober‑friendly space offering alcohol-free dance events and wellness workshops. Visit sanctuarympls.org.

💬 Sober Raver Social Groups & Support

  • Sober Rave Discord MN – Peer support space for sober ravers to share events, tips, or just hang. Ask for an invite via the MN Sober Ravers Facebook group.
  • Meetup: Minnesota Sober Dance & Wellness – Group organizing sober hikes, yoga, dance parties. Search “Minnesota Sober Dance” on Meetup.com.
  • Minnesota Recovery Connection – Peer-led support organization hosting sober social events and recovery resources across the Twin Cities. Visit mnrecovery.org.
  • SMART Recovery Minnesota – Evidence-based, self-empowerment focused addiction recovery meetings across metro and suburbs. See smartrecovery.org/minnesota.
  • Celebrate Recovery at Grace Church – Inclusive support group providing social events and sponsor networks in Minneapolis. Info: gracechurchmpls.org.
  • Adult Children of Alcoholics (ACA) – MN Chapter – Support for individuals healing from family-related trauma. Visit adultchildren.org.
  • Alano Club of Minneapolis – Sober social club downtown hosting daily AA/NA/Al‑Anon meetings plus communal activities. Visit alanoclubmpls.org.
  • Minnesota Harm Reduction Action Center – While focused on harm reduction, they host sober space circles and peer events occasionally. Visit mnharc.org.

🍹 Top-Rated Mocktail Recipes

  • Electric Lemonade: Lemonade, non-alcoholic blue curaçao syrup, sparkling water, lemon slice. Sweet, tangy, and glows under blacklight.
  • Ginger Citrus Fizz: Ginger beer, fresh lime juice, orange, mint leaves. Zesty and energizing.
  • Virgin Mojito: Lime, mint, sugar, soda water. Sparkling and hydrating.
  • Cucumber Cooler: Cucumber juice, lime, simple syrup, tonic water. Crisp and refreshing.
