Verified NA Shops & Bars

Minneapolis (3506 Nicollet Ave) & St. Paul (2236 Carter Ave)

Both locations are active, open Mon–Sat 12–6 PM, and completely dedicated to non-alcoholic beer, spirits, wine, cocktail mixers, THC drinks, and NA merch.

💡 Bonus: They house ~60 THC drink varieties and continue hosting tastings and pop-ups.

Visit Marigold

Opened March 15, 2024, and still active as Minnesota's first sober nightclub. Events continue monthly with DJs, mocktails, live entertainment, karaoke, and community meet-ups.
