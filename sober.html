Top-Rated Mocktail Recipes

Craft delicious alcohol-free drinks perfect for pre-rave gatherings or enjoying at home. These recipes are crowd-pleasers that look and taste impressive without the alcohol.

Electric Lemonade

A vibrant, glowing mocktail perfect for rave parties. The blue cura√ßao syrup gives it that electric blue color that glows under blacklight!

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice
  2. Pour 6 oz fresh lemonade (homemade or quality store-bought)
  3. Add 1 oz non-alcoholic blue cura√ßao syrup (available at specialty stores or online)
  4. Top with 2 oz sparkling water or club soda
  5. Stir gently to create a gradient effect
  6. Garnish with a lemon slice and edible glitter

Pro tip: For blacklight parties, add a few drops of tonic water (which contains quinine) to make it glow!

Ginger Citrus Fizz

A refreshing, spicy-sweet mocktail with a kick. The ginger beer provides natural energy and aids digestion.

  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Add 4 oz quality ginger beer (Fever-Tree or Reed's work well)
  3. Squeeze 1 oz fresh lime juice
  4. Add 1 oz fresh orange juice
  5. Drop in a few fresh mint leaves and gently muddle
  6. Stir lightly and garnish with an orange wedge and mint sprig

Variation: For extra spice, add a slice of fresh jalape√±o before muddling.

Virgin Mojito

A classic, refreshing mocktail that's perfect for warm weather. The mint provides a natural energy boost.

  1. Muddle 8-10 fresh mint leaves with 1 tsp sugar (or simple syrup) in a glass
  2. Add juice of 1 lime (about 1 oz)
  3. Fill the glass with crushed ice
  4. Top with soda water or club soda
  5. Stir well from the bottom up to distribute mint
  6. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wedge

Tip: Use brown sugar for a deeper, more caramel-like flavor.

PLUR Punch

A party-friendly punch that embodies the PLUR (Peace, Love, Unity, Respect) spirit. Perfect for group gatherings.

  1. In a punch bowl or large pitcher, mix 4 cups cranberry juice with 2 cups pineapple juice
  2. Add 1 cup fresh lime juice
  3. Stir in 3 cups ginger ale or sparkling water
  4. Add ice and fruit slices (oranges, limes, berries)
  5. Garnish with lime wheels and edible glitter for rave-ready presentation

For a crowd: This recipe serves 8-10. Double or triple as needed!
