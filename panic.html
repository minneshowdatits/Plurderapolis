Emergency Help

This page is here for when shit gets real. Stay calm. You’ve got this. Let’s get through it together.

Overdose Response

  • Check if they’re breathing or conscious — shout, shake gently
  • Call 911 — say “My friend isn’t breathing” (not “they’re high”)
  • Use Narcan if available — spray 1 dose in their nose, wait 2-3 mins
  • If no response, give 2nd dose + start rescue breathing
  • Stay until help arrives — Good Samaritan laws protect you in MN

Mental Health & Support

📞 Call 988 - Mental Health Crisis Line 📱 Text HOME to 741741 - Crisis Text Line

Sexual Assault & Crisis Support

📞 Call 1-800-656-HOPE (National Sexual Assault Hotline) 📞 Call 612-825-HELP (MN Sexual Violence Crisis Line) 📱 Text 612825HELP (MN Crisis Text Line)

If you or someone you know has been assaulted, help is here 24/7.

Poison Control

📞 Call 1-800-222-1222 - Poison Control Hotline

Safe Ride Options

  • Use rideshare apps like Uber or Lyft with safety check-ins enabled
  • Look for local sober ride programs if available
  • Don’t hesitate to ask venue staff for help getting safe transportation

911 Call Tips

  • Be calm and clear
  • Give your location precisely
  • Describe the emergency briefly and factually
  • Say exactly what you see — avoid judgmental language

Venue Safety & Bystander Moves

  • Find staff or security and ask for water or medical help
  • Most venues have chill zones and medical stations
  • If someone is unconscious, place them in the recovery position if safe to do so
  • If harassment happens, calmly interrupt or get someone who can
