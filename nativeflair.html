Accessibility Features

Our website includes several accessibility features to improve your experience:

  • Text Size: Adjust the text size to make it easier to read

These controls are always available in the accessibility panel. You can move it around the screen or minimize it as needed.

About Native Flair

Native Flair is the creative passion of a talented artist who finds beauty in transforming ordinary vases into extraordinary works of art. Despite visual challenges, she pours her heart and soul into each piece, creating stunning interior paintings that captivate and inspire.

Every vase tells a story, with colors and patterns that reflect nature's beauty and the artist's unique perspective. The intricate designs are carefully painted on the inside of each vase, creating a delightful surprise when viewed from different angles.

Tribal Inspiration

The Saginaw Chippewa Indian Tribe of Michigan has a rich cultural heritage that inspires the Native Flair collection. Based in the Great Lakes region, the tribe's traditional artistry, beadwork, and connection to nature influence many of the designs.

Traditional Anishinaabe floral patterns, woodland imagery, and earth-toned color palettes pay homage to the tribe's artistic traditions while creating contemporary pieces for modern homes.

Each vase incorporates elements inspired by Michigan's natural landscapes - the flowing rivers, forest textures, and seasonal changes that have shaped the Saginaw people's way of life for generations.

Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Native Flair is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Measures to Support Accessibility

Native Flair takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices
  • Appoint an accessibility officer and/or ombudsperson
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff
  • Employ formal accessibility quality assurance methods

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. Native Flair is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.
