Our Story

Native Flair is the creative passion of a talented artist who finds beauty in transforming ordinary vases into extraordinary works of art. Despite visual challenges, she pours her heart and soul into each piece, creating stunning interior paintings that captivate and inspire.

Every vase tells a story, with colors and patterns that reflect nature's beauty and the artist's unique perspective. The intricate designs are carefully painted on the inside of each vase, creating a delightful surprise when viewed from different angles.

Our work is inspired by the rich artistic traditions of the Saginaw Chippewa Indian Tribe, while also incorporating contemporary design elements. As an independent artist, we honor these traditions with respect and admiration, celebrating the beauty of Native American art forms.

Tribal Inspiration

The Saginaw Chippewa Indian Tribe of Michigan has a rich cultural heritage that inspires the Native Flair collection. Based in the Great Lakes region, the tribe's traditional artistry, beadwork, and connection to nature influence many of the designs.

Traditional Anishinaabe floral patterns, woodland imagery, and earth-toned color palettes pay homage to the tribe's artistic traditions while creating contemporary pieces for modern homes.

Each vase incorporates elements inspired by Michigan's natural landscapes - the flowing rivers, forest textures, and seasonal changes that have shaped the Saginaw people's way of life for generations.

Note: While our work is inspired by Saginaw Chippewa traditions, we are an independent artist respectfully drawing from these cultural influences rather than official tribal representatives.
