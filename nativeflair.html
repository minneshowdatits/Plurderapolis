About Native Flair

Our Story

Native Flair was founded in 2015 by Maria Littlefeather, a ceramic artist with deep roots in indigenous traditions. Each vase is handcrafted using techniques passed down through generations.

Meet the Artist

Maria combines contemporary design with traditional patterns, creating pieces that honor her heritage while fitting modern interiors.

Honoring Ancestral Roots

Our designs draw inspiration from various indigenous cultures, with careful research and respect for the symbolism behind each pattern.
