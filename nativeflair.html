About Native Flair

Native Flair is the creative passion of one talented Native American artist based in Minnesota. As a one-woman operation, she pours her heart and soul into transforming ordinary objects into extraordinary works of art.

Specializing in upcycling trinkets and jewelry, she breathes new life into forgotten pieces while honoring traditional Native American artistry. Her creative talents extend beyond vases to include painting, costume design, jewelry making, and more.

Custom Commissions

Request a Custom Piece

Looking for something unique? Native Flair accepts custom commission work for special occasions, gifts, or to match your specific home decor.

Commissioned pieces start at $75 and can include personalized colors, patterns, sizes, and design elements. Each custom vase comes with a certificate of authenticity and the story behind its creation.

Our Creative Process

Design Inspiration

Each design begins with inspiration from nature, tribal patterns, and colors that speak to the artist's heritage and creative vision.

Interior Painting

Using specialized techniques, the interior of each vase is carefully painted with precision, often requiring multiple layers and careful attention.

Curing Process

The painted vases undergo a careful curing process to ensure durability and longevity of the artwork, preserving it for generations.

Careful Packaging

Each vase is carefully packaged with eco-friendly materials to ensure it arrives safely at your doorstep, ready to display.
