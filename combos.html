Common Minnesota Drug Combos

These aren’t recommendations. These are *real combos* we see in the scene—and what you need to know to stay safer if you’re going to do them anyway.

Weed + Xanax

Chill until it’s *too* chill. This combo can hit heavy if your tolerance isn’t high. Risk of blacking out, memory loss, and increased respiratory depression. If you're nodding out—skip the crossfade.

Molly + 2C-B

Common at raves and DIYs. Usually called “candy flipping’s spicy cousin.” Roll first, wait ~30 mins before dropping the 2C-B. Don’t mix both at once unless you know your body well. Overheating and serotonin syndrome are real.

Ketamine + Alcohol

Popular but risky AF. Both are depressants, and combo-ing can make you spin out hard. Blackouts and physical collapse aren’t rare. Take one or the other—or wait until the K wears off before drinking again.

Adderall + MDMA

Speedballing without opiates. Super common among rave kids who want to stay up and party longer—but MDMA + stimulants increase risk of heart issues and serotonin syndrome. Stay hydrated, take breaks, and **don’t redose** MDMA while on stims.

Mushrooms + Nitrous

Classic trippy stack. Nitrous (whippits) can spike visuals but also drop your oxygen and make you pass out. If you're doing both, sit down, breathe deep between hits, and don't hog the canister like a dick.
