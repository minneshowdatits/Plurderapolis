🧠 Polydrug Risks

Mixing substances = way less predictable, way more dangerous. Here’s what to know before you stack.

⚡ Stimulant + Stimulant

Examples: MDMA + Cocaine, Amphetamine + MDA
Risks: Heart attack, seizures, serotonin syndrome, psychosis, overheating.

💤 Depressant + Depressant

Examples: Alcohol + Benzos, GHB + Oxy
Risks: Respiratory arrest, coma, blackouts, overdose.

⚠️ Stimulant + Depressant

Examples: Cocaine + Alcohol, MDMA + GHB
Risks: Liver strain, dehydration, unpredictable crashes.

🌈 Stimulant + Psychedelic

Examples: LSD + MDMA (candyflipping)
Risks: Panic, confusion, serotonin syndrome.

🌪️ Depressant + Psychedelic

Examples: Alcohol + Shrooms
Risks: Amnesia, sedation, unpredictable outcomes.

🧊 Ketamine Combos

Examples: K + Alcohol, K + Coke
Risks: Blackouts, vomiting, injury, overdose.

☠️ Opioids + Depressants

Examples: Heroin + Benzos, Fentanyl + Alcohol
Risks: Most lethal combo. High OD risk.

🧬 Cocaine + Alcohol

Creates: Cocaethylene
Risks: Heart and liver toxicity, seizures.
