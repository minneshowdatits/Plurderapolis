💊 Rx + Party Drug Warnings

Here’s what to watch for when you’re prescribed psych meds but still plan to party:

SSRI/SNRI Antidepressants

  • + MDMA → dulled high, risk of redosing, serotonin syndrome
  • + LSD/shrooms → unpredictable visuals, emotional instability
  • + Stimulants → overstimulation, anxiety, hypertension

Wellbutrin (Bupropion)

  • + MDMA/Coke → lowered seizure threshold, anxiety, panic
  • + Alcohol → blackouts, aggression, disinhibition

Benzos + Downers

  • + Opiates/Alcohol → high risk of fatal overdose
  • + GHB/Ket → blackout, loss of motor control

Gabapentin / Lyrica

  • + Alcohol/Opiates → deep sedation, memory gaps, high OD risk

ADHD Meds

  • + MDMA/Coke → serotonin overload, stroke, heart issues
  • + Alcohol → blackouts, risky behavior
  • + Psychedelics → destabilization, anxiety

Antipsychotics

  • + Psychedelics → muted trips, psychosis risk, tremors
  • + MDMA → dull euphoria, risk of redosing

Lithium

  • + Psychedelics → seizures, unpredictable reactions, fatalities reported

Sleep Meds (Hydroxyzine, Trazodone)

  • + Alcohol/Downers → blackout, sedation, fall risk

🛟 Golden Rules

  • Don’t stack downers. (Xanax + alcohol = coma)
  • Don’t stack uppers. (Adderall + coke = heart attack)
  • Test everything. Every time. Even pills from friends.
  • Psychedelics aren’t just visuals—your whole mind shifts
  • Have a trusted friend nearby, always
  • Start low, especially if you’re new, on meds, or recently sober
