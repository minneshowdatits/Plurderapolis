ID & Immigration Status in Minnesota

Clear answers to common questions to reduce misinformation and fear.

Q: Do I have to carry ID in Minnesota?

Generally no, unless you are driving or have been lawfully detained by police.

Q: Can ICE enter my home?

Only with a judicial warrant signed by a judge OR with consent.

Protest & Demonstration Safety

People in Minnesota have rights when protesting. Knowing these basics can reduce risk.

Your Rights:

  • Right to Protest: Legal on sidewalks, parks, and public spaces
  • Right to Record: Can record police from safe distance
  • Right to Remain Silent: Do not have to answer questions
ACLU Protest Guide

Digital & Phone Safety

Protect your privacy before, during, and after events.

  • Use Strong Passcode: Instead of biometrics in sensitive situations
  • Manage Location: Turn off location sharing for sensitive apps
  • Secure Communications: Use encrypted messaging like Signal
  • Know Your Rights: Don't have to unlock phone without warrant
Privacy Tip: Store important numbers in a secure note app.
