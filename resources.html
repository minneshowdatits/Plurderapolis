🧭 Minnesota Resources

Local orgs, clinics, and community hubs that care. These aren’t just numbers — they’re lifelines. You deserve safety, respect, and privacy every step of the way.

🆘 Emergency & Crisis Quick Reference
  • 911 – For all immediate emergencies
  • Poison Control – 1-800-222-1222
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline – 988
  • Crisis Text Line – Text MN to 741741 for 24/7 mental health support
  • Day One Crisis Line – 1-866-223-1111 (Domestic violence, sexual assault help)
  • Steve Rummler HOPE Network – Narcan and fentanyl test strips mailed to your door anywhere in MN. Training and advocacy too. Visit Site
  • Southside Harm Reduction – Free syringes, test strips, gear, and support in South Minneapolis. southsideharmreduction.org
  • Family Tree Clinic – Low-barrier, queer-inclusive care. Narcan, STI testing, and safer sex kits. Visit Site
  • Dose of Reality MN – Find Narcan and test strip access across Minnesota. Visit Site
  • Rural AIDS Action Network (RAAN) – Free Narcan, test strips, and HIV/STI testing in rural and greater MN. Visit Site
  • Red Door Clinic – Free/sliding scale STI and HIV testing, PEP/PrEP, respectful staff. Visit Site
  • JustUs Health – HIV/STI testing, gender-affirming care, and PrEP navigation. Visit Site
  • Neighborhood HealthSource – Sliding scale primary care and sexual health services. Visit Site
  • COPE Mobile Crisis Team (Hennepin County) – 24/7 crisis response team. Call 612-596-1223
  • Crisis Text Line – Text MN to 741741
  • Walk-In Counseling Center – Free, no-appointment counseling. Visit Site
  • RECLAIM – Mental health support for queer and trans youth. Sliding scale, affirming care. Visit Site
  • Family Tree Clinic – Trans health care, queer-safe services. Visit Site
  • OutFront Minnesota – LGBTQ+ legal, advocacy, and anti-violence support. Visit Site
  • BEAM – Black Emotional and Mental Health Collective. Visit Site
  • Southside Harm Reduction – Syringes, safer smoking supplies, gear, and use education. Visit Site
  • RAAN – Syringe access and HIV outreach in outstate MN. Visit Site
  • Minnesota Recovery Connection – Peer recovery coaching, sober community events, and resource navigation. Visit Site
  • The Bridge for Youth – Safe shelter, support, and housing for youth in crisis. Visit Site
  • YouthLink MN – Drop-in center and holistic support for youth experiencing homelessness. Visit Site

If you're being hurt, threatened, manipulated, or violated — there are people ready to listen, believe you, and help you get safe. No judgment. No pressure. Just support.

  • Day One Crisis Line – Call 1-866-223-1111 or visit dayoneservices.org
  • Sexual Violence Center (SVC) – Call 612-871-5111 or visit sexualviolencecenter.org
  • Cornerstone MN – cornerstonemn.org
  • Aurora Center (U of M) – aurora.umn.edu
  • MNCASA – mncasa.org
  • OutFront Minnesota – outfront.org
  • SWOP Minneapolis – Mutual aid, advocacy, and support. swopusa.org/chapter/minneapolis
  • The Cupcake Girls (MN Chapter) – Harm reduction, therapy, and resources for sex workers. thecupcakegirls.org
🔒 Confidentiality & Privacy: All services respect your privacy and confidentiality. Especially if you’re youth, queer, or sex working, your safety and anonymity matter. Don’t hesitate to ask about confidentiality when you reach out.
