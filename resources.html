Minnesota Resources

Community-based support for harm reduction, mental health, safety, and survival.

💊 Harm Reduction & Overdose Prevention

  • Steve Rummler HOPE Network – Free Narcan & fentanyl test strips
  • Southside Harm Reduction – Syringes, Narcan, testing, safe use supplies
  • Harm Reduction Sisters – Northern MN delivery & drop-in harm reduction
  • Aliveness Project – Mobile syringe exchange van in the Twin Cities
  • NorthPoint Health – Testing, supplies, overdose education

🌸 Reproductive Health & Plan B Access

  • Just The Pill – Free/low-cost Plan B and abortion-by-mail (MN-based)
  • Our Justice – Abortion funding, Plan B, and clinic support
  • Planned Parenthood North Central States – Emergency contraception, STI testing, more
  • RECLAIM – Queer-affirming mental & sexual health support

💔 Sexual & Domestic Violence Support

  • Cornerstone – 24/7 crisis line, shelter, legal help: 1-866-223-1111
  • Violence Free Minnesota – Statewide help & shelter locator
  • The Bridge for Youth – Emergency housing and support for youth
  • Sexual Violence Center – Support groups, crisis lines, and advocacy

🏳️‍⚧️ LGBTQ+ Affirming Support

  • RECLAIM – Therapy and healing for queer and trans youth
  • Transforming Families MN – Support for trans people and their loved ones
  • The Aliveness Project – LGBTQ+ friendly HIV resources and holistic care
  • Family Tree Clinic – Sliding scale trans-inclusive reproductive health

🧠 Crisis & Mental Health

  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • COPE (Hennepin County) – 612-596-1223
  • Fast Tracker – Mental health + substance use provider search tool
  • NAMI HelpLine – Peer mental health guidance

🔄 Peer Support & Recovery

  • Minnesota Recovery Connection – Peer coaching, recovery community center, statewide events
  • Peer Support Alliance – Advocacy and training for peer recovery specialists in MN
  • Families Anonymous – Peer support for loved ones of people struggling with substance use
  • Unity Recovery (Virtual) – Free, online, peer-led recovery groups multiple times a day
  • Southside Harm Reduction – Community care, no abstinence required

📦 Shelter, Food & Basic Needs

  • United Way 211 – Call/text 211 for emergency food, housing, and resources
  • The Bridge for Youth – Youth shelter and housing support
  • Coordinated Entry (Hennepin) – Housing support system
