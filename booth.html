What Our Booths Provide

Comprehensive harm reduction services to ensure your event is safe and supportive for all attendees

💊

Overdose Prevention

We provide free naloxone (Narcan) kits, fentanyl test strips, and overdose response training to prevent tragedies.

💧

Hydration Support

Water stations, electrolyte supplements, and cooling areas to prevent dehydration and heat-related issues.

🛋️

Safe Spaces

Chill-out areas with seating, ear protection, and calming resources for attendees feeling overwhelmed.

📚

Education & Resources

Honest information about substance safety, consent culture, mental health resources, and community care.

🛡️

Safety Resources

Basic first aid, safe escorts to vehicles, and connections to medical or additional support services when needed.

❤️

Peer Support

Trained volunteers offering non-judgmental support, active listening, and de-escalation techniques.

About PLURderapolis

Driven by community need

Since 2019, PLURderapolis has been providing essential harm reduction services at Minnesota's electronic music events. We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy music events safely, with dignity, and without judgment.

Our trained volunteers create safer spaces where party-goers can access resources, education, and support. We meet people where they're at, offering practical tools for risk reduction while fostering a culture of care and community responsibility.
