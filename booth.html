What We Provide at Our Booths

Our trained volunteers set up a free, safe booth at your event to keep the community safe and glowing. We offer:

  • Free naloxone (Narcan) and overdose response supplies
  • Fentanyl test strips and pill testing resources
  • Water & electrolytes to stay hydrated
  • Gum, mints, and simple snacks
  • Hair ties, hygiene items, and basic care products
  • Earplugs and a chill space to rest
  • Safe escorts to your car if you feel unsafe
  • Peer support, resources, and friendly conversation

About Us

PLURderapolis has been setting up harm reduction and safer-space booths at Minnesota shows and festivals since 2019. We've spent years learning what helps most at events and building trust with the community. Our goal is simple: make nightlife safer, kinder, and more connected.

Originally known as Project Peach, we've provided booths at warehouse shows, clubs, and events like Intellephunk's The Great Beyond, collaborating with local collectives to support Minnesota's electronic music community [citation:1].
